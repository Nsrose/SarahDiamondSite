About Me
I'm a rising senior at American University excited to build a career in business development. I am passionate about working for organizations that focus on environmental awareness, music and the arts, youth advocacy, and education.

During my undergraduate studies I've lived in Washington D.C. and London to attend the London School of Economics. My professional experience is in business development so I have a strong background in marketing, fundraising, communications and event planning. My favorite aspect of the administrative positions I've held has been integrating youth education into my position. Whether working in a music store, at a nature conservancy, or Hebrew school, I have always treasured the opportunities I have to work directly with kids who are excited to learn.

I am eager to apply my skills in community outreach and business development to future opportunities.
